about us

Dental AI was founded by a team of experienced researchers from the University of Jordan. With a focus on developing and improving their product, the School of Engineering, and especially the Computer Engineering Department, collaborated with the School of Dentistry to conduct extensive research. By utilizing the latest technologies and analyzing large amounts of data, they were able to create a highly accurate and reliable application that takes diagnosing patients to the next level. The team's primary goal was to provide dentists with the best possible tool to diagnose and treat their patients. By ensuring the highest level of accuracy, Dental AI aims to provide a valuable second opinion that complements the expertise of dental professionals.
